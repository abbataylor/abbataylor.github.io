About Me

Hi, I'm Abba Moses — a passionate Full-Stack Web Developer and IT Solutions Specialist based in Kampala, Uganda. I'm currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering at Kampala International University, following a completed Diploma in Information Technology.

I specialise in building modern, responsive, and high-performing web applications using PHP (Laravel), MySQL, JavaScript, TypeScript, HTML, and CSS. I've delivered 13+ real-world projects — from enterprise platforms like a real estate system and a careers portal, to data analysis tools and polished frontend components.

As the founder of AbbaSoft, my goal is to deliver smart, scalable, and user-focused IT solutions — from business automation tools to creative, brand-driven websites — all built with precision and clean code.

💡 What Drives Me

I believe technology should make life simpler, empower people, and help businesses grow. My mission is to design and build solutions that do exactly that — beautifully and intelligently. I'm actively looking for opportunities where I can grow as a developer, contribute meaningfully, and build software that matters.
