About Me

Hi, I’m Abba Moses — a passionate Full-Stack Web Developer and IT Solutions Specialist based in Kampala, Uganda. I’m currently pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Software Engineering, following my Diploma in Information Technology.

I specialize in building modern, responsive, and high-performing websites and web applications using PHP (Laravel), MySQL, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. My experience includes developing professional systems such as a Cargo Shipping Platform, an IT Support Chatbot, and a Student Clearance System.

As the founder of AbbaSoft, my goal is to deliver smart, scalable, and user-focused IT solutions — from business automation tools to creative, brand-driven websites — all built with precision and clean code.

💡 What Drives Me

I believe technology should make life simpler, empower people, and help businesses grow. My mission is to design solutions that do exactly that — beautifully and intelligently.
